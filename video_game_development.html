Video Game development

Final project - video game "Distorsión"
3D platform game developed on Unity3D.
Executable of the game. (83,8 MB)

Synopsis:

Anna is a girl who usually spends her afternoons entertaining herself with her father's laboratory equipment since she lost her mother a few years ago because of illness. However, today is an important day: Anna's father needs her help to finish the latest invention and Anna cannot be distracted.

Once it get started, a great explosion is originated and that will cause our protagonist to be the only one capable of solving the mysteries around the machine and her father.
