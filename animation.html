Technical explanations from "Tras la valla"

Quaternions
The video displays different implementations of the movement of a coach on a roller coaster. Using Python to develop a plug-in for Blender.

Kinematic Chain
We can watch in the video the use of skeletons to which forward and inverse kinematics are applied.

Motion Capture
In this video is used a Motion Capture system to show two scenes, made by different techniques of adjustment between geometry and the real actor (MOCAP's data).

Motion capture data processing
In the video is displayed how the MOCAP's data is processed and how the choice of the threshold point affects it.
